Project Arrythmia

Project Arrythmia is a musical bullet-hell video game wherein players are to avoid colored projectiles that move depending on the beat of the song played.

Facts About Project Arrythmia

  • Created by Vitamin Games in 2019
  • Still currently being developed and updated
  • Mostly community driven (like Geometry Dash)
  • Often compaired to Just Shapes & Beats,
    a game with similar gameplay

Why I Enjoy Playing Project Arrthymia

  1. Community: Players are able to create and upload their
    levels, or play the works of other creators.
  2. Musical Element: The bullet hell mixed with the rhymic gimick
    provides fun and engaging gameplay that not many other games have.
